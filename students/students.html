Academics & Learning
Class Schedules: The official lecture periods run from 7:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday.
Learning Hubs: The university provides a robust online Student Portal, an extensive library holding both print and digital resources, and a specialized eCampus.
Practical Learning: Students engage in active community-based projects and attend career-focused lectures hosted by industry professionals and entrepreneurs.

Campus Life & Support
Accommodation: All students are admitted on a non-residential basis. The university assists in connecting learners with housing, but off-campus living builds independence.
Student Services: Support systems include bursary/loan guidance, psychologists, counseling, and a Special Needs Section located in the main library.
Extracurriculars: College life includes faith groups, sporting activities, and community engagement clubs that build friendships and networks.

Flexible Learning Options
eCampus: The UNIMA eCampus allows distance learners to study at their own pace from anywhere in Malawi and beyond, supporting career growth alongside studies.
